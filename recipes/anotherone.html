Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread (toasted)
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • Salt, pepper, and a squeeze of lemon

Directions

  1. Toast the bread to your liking.
  2. Mash the avocado and season with salt, pepper, and lemon.
  3. Spread the avocado on toast and serve immediately.
