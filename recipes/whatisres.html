Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed seasonal fruit (berries, apple, banana, orange)
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Directions

  1. Chop larger fruits into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Toss fruit with honey and lemon juice to combine.
  3. Chill 10 minutes and serve fresh.
