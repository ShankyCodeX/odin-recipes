Lasagna Recipe

Home

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Brown the beef in a skillet and drain excess fat.
  3. Layer noodles, sauce, ricotta, beef, and mozzarella in a baking dish.
  4. Bake for 35–45 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  5. Let rest 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
